Week Two Final App Project Write-up: Requirements

Version 0.2.0 - Initial

As mentioned during Week 1, you need to be working on an Ionic app as your final project for this course.
The app doesn't have to be very fancy.
It should serve as a demonstration of the concepts we learned during the course.
More importantly, I would like this app to be something that you can expand or use in real life after you have completed this course.

It's *OK* to build another chat app or tweet app. But, ask yourself, do you really want to re-invent the wheel?

Here are some of the things to keep in mind that are requirements for this app:

1. Yes, it needs to be an Ionic app. Preferably, it will be tested on real mobile device (iOS or Android).
  If you can't get hold of a device, that's OK.

2. It should use at least one of the Cordova Native plugins found on https://ionicframework.com/docs/native/Links to an external site..

3. Preferably, the app also uses MongoDB. If it is not relevant to your app, that's OK.

4. It's also preferable to utilize Heroku service.
  Again, if it is not an option, that's fine too.

5. If you cannot implement all the features that you envisioned for this app by end of the week 8, that is perfectly fine.
  But, please give some documentation of what you implemented and what you couldn't.
  And, share if you have some plans to implement in the future.

6. Final course app should be submitted into GitHub repository using GitHub Classroom.
  This is the link to the Final app assignment:
  https://classroom.github.com/a/vl9UEny0

Note: Please push the code you started on the final app as often as possible to allow for the instructor to review the work you are doing along the way.

Week Two Final App Project: Write-up

1. Corporate Project Proposals provides a Resource for Management of Internal Research & Development (IRaD), Sustainment, Lab Revitalization & Maintenance,
Capital Investments, Innovation, Ideation, and Change Mangement!


2. Storyboard: CPP will provide an app for submitting proposals for internal corporate infrastructure improvements to support work conducted by the organization.
CPP will support three main use cases & personas, end users for proposal submission, middle management and or staff that may evaluate, process and prioritization,
and investment board decision makers who provide award/grant. This app would include simple functions (proposal form submission), dashboard status display,
and complex task (proposal evaluation, prioritization, scoring, selection for award/grant).


3. Proposals may include sustainment, revitalization, and maintenance, or establishment of a new facility or capability.


4. Potential Tabs for the App:
  Home - Introduction and High Level introduction to the App and Category Areas
  Categories - Details of each Category
  Submit Form - Submit form to upload a new proposal, including images from device camera to include in proposal
  Dashboard - List of existing proposals
  About - Information at this final project assignment
  Contact - Information about creator of assignment/app and school information


5. Potential Categories for this App:
  Maintenance - Facilities
  Maintenance - Shops
  Capital - Equipment
  Capital - Small Construction
  Capital - Large Construction
